# Peter's Digest Video Prompt - 2026-03-08

## Story Selection

**Story:** The "Code Deployment" Drone Strike
- **Hook:** Amazon claims a simple code error caused a massive shopping outage—right as Iranian drones were literally blowing up their data centers in the Middle East.
- **Source:** AI Ecosystem Positioning & Screening Candidates (Amazon)
- **Why it works:** **Intellectual novelty / anomaly.** The absurd coincidence of corporate PR deflecting to a "code deployment" while their physical infrastructure is literally taking kinetic fire creates an immediate "wait, what?" reaction. It hits the "Satirical Expertise Pattern" by highlighting corporate deflection.

**Story:** BlackRock Closes the Vault
- **Hook:** BlackRock manages over ten trillion dollars, but if you want your money out of their flagship private credit fund right now, you have to wait.
- **Source:** Screening Candidates (BlackRock)
- **Why it works:** **Social value & High-arousal emotion (fear).** It leverages striking data (a $10T manager freezing redemptions after hitting 9.3%) and positions the viewer as an insider who sees the "canary in the coal mine" for the broader credit cycle before the mainstream catches on.

**Story:** Nike's Secret Hiring Spree
- **Hook:** Nike's stock is down 20% and everyone assumes the brand is in freefall, but alternative data shows they just quietly spiked their hiring by 79% in a single week.
- **Source:** Screening Candidates (Nike)
- **Why it works:** **Intellectual novelty & Social value.** It breaks a known rule (failing companies usually lay people off, they don't aggressively hire) and gives the audience an exclusive turnaround signal that makes them look smart for sharing.

**Story:** The Stagflation Reality Check
- **Hook:** The US just unexpectedly lost 92,000 jobs right as Qatar warned that the Middle East conflict could shut down global energy production within weeks.
- **Source:** Market & Macro Overview & Reddit Top Posts
- **Why it works:** **Personal value & High-arousal emotion.** It connects two massive macroeconomic shocks (the jobs miss and oil surging past $90/bbl) to the viewer's immediate economic reality, tapping into genuine fears about stagflation.

## Script

> **(Scene 1 — The Amazon "Code" Deployment)**
> **Narrator:** Amazon just suffered a massive shopping outage, but don't worry, they said it was just a routine code deployment error. Which is technically true, if the code was deployed by an Iranian drone. At the exact same time as the US outage, Amazon's physical data centers in the UAE and Bahrain were taking kinetic fire from drone strikes.

> **(Scene 2 — The BlackRock Vault)**
> **Narrator:** Meanwhile, BlackRock — managing over ten trillion dollars — needs a little more time with your money. Their flagship private credit fund just hit a redemption wall. Investors tried to pull out over nine percent of the fund, way past the five percent limit. Apparently ten trillion dollars doesn't go as far as it used to when private credit dries up.

> **(Scene 3 — Nike's Secret Spree)**
> **Narrator:** If you want a weird signal, look at Nike. Their stock is down twenty percent and everyone thinks the brand is dying. But alternative data just caught them quietly surging their active job postings by almost eighty percent in a single week. You don't double your hiring budget when you're preparing for the end.

> **(Scene 4 — The Stagflation Barrel)**
> **Narrator:** Finally, the US unexpectedly shed ninety-two thousand jobs in February. Combine that with Qatar warning that global energy production could shut down in weeks and oil crossing ninety dollars a barrel. The market isn't just worried about a recession anymore; it's staring straight down the barrel of stagflation.

## Visual Prompts

**Guidelines:** 
- **Style:** Miniature Model / Diorama.
- **Aesthetic:** Small-scale handcrafted physical miniature sets, tiny figures made as miniature models, visible miniature craft textures, tabletop diorama.
- **Session-Specific Decision:** Consolidated to one primary visual/video prompt per story to cover the full narration block.

> **Scene 1 — The Amazon "Code" Deployment**
> - **Image Prompt:** A tabletop diorama of a massive, sterile Amazon data center made of grey plastic miniature server racks. Half of the server racks are violently exploding with orange and yellow handcrafted cotton-wool fire and tiny broken plastic debris. Hovering above the destruction is a small, pristine delivery drone gently lowering a single cardboard box labeled "CODE" onto the burning wreckage. Small-scale handcrafted physical miniature set, visible miniature craft textures, dramatic miniature studio lighting.
> - **Video Prompt:** Slow push-in on the burning data center diorama. The cotton-wool smoke effects remain static while the delivery drone slowly lowers the "CODE" box into the flames.

> **Scene 2 — The BlackRock Vault**
> - **Image Prompt:** A tiny handmade miniature scale model of a colossal, imposing bank vault door painted in dark metallic tones. The massive steel door is bulging outward, visibly straining under immense pressure from inside, but it is being held completely shut by a single, flimsy piece of clear Scotch tape stretching across the gap. A tiny figure in a suit stands outside holding a "closed" sign. Small-scale handcrafted physical miniature set, tabletop diorama, visible miniature craft textures.
> - **Video Prompt:** Slow, dramatic pull-back from the heavily bulging vault door. The metal door groans and shifts slightly under the pressure, but the single piece of Scotch tape stubbornly holds it shut. 

> **Scene 3 — Nike's Secret Spree**
> - **Image Prompt:** A tabletop diorama of a dilapidated, sinking Nike storefront, tilted downward into a miniature cracked pavement street. Despite the crumbling exterior, the back door is propped open, and a massive, comically long line of tiny miniature figures in business casual attire are eagerly flooding into the building carrying resumes. Small-scale handcrafted physical miniature set, tiny figures made as miniature models, visible miniature craft textures.
> - **Video Prompt:** Static wide shot of the sinking storefront. The camera holds steady while the long line of tiny applicant figures slowly and steadily shuffles forward into the back door of the crumbling building.

> **Scene 4 — The Stagflation Barrel**
> - **Image Prompt:** A small-scale handcrafted physical miniature set showing a vintage, rusty gas pump with the price display spinning out of control. Tied directly to the gas pump's nozzle is a tiny, frustrated miniature worker figure holding a pink "Pink Slip" termination paper. The entire scene is placed precariously on the edge of a steep miniature cliff made of carved foam. Tiny handmade miniature scale models, visible miniature craft textures, moody and dim diorama lighting.
> - **Video Prompt:** Slow pan around the rusty gas pump. The price numbers on the pump rapidly spin higher and higher while the tiny worker figure dangles on the edge of the cliff.
