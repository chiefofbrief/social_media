## Step 2: The Script

**STEP 2 SCRIPT QUESTIONS:**
*   **Audience & Tone:** Sharp, urgent, and focused on the scale mismatch of the numbers.
*   **Rules Check:** Front-loaded hooks, no filler words, two-sentence body blocks, and specific figures ($60B, 900%, $25B, 50k).
*   **The Outro:** "Keep your eye on the boring stuff" frames the massive costs of physical hardware and fleet logistics as the real market signals.

**TTS Prompt Style Instruction:** Read like a financial analyst delivering news at a fast pace.

**(Scene 1 — The Hook)**
Narrator: Thirty-percent gas spikes, nine-hundred percent growth that isn't enough for Wall Street, and why Uber is betting a billion dollars your next driver isn't human.

**(Scene 2 — The Qatar LNG Inferno)**
Narrator: A missile strike crippled twenty percent of the world’s liquefied natural gas at Qatar’s massive sixty-billion-dollar export hub. It took twenty-five years to build, and it’ll take at least five more to fix the damage.

**(Scene 3 — The Micron Growth Paradox)**
Narrator: Micron delivered staggering nine-hundred-percent earnings growth, yet the stock is falling because AI success requires a twenty-five-billion-dollar hardware bill. Record-breaking profits can't outrun the terrifying cost of building the physical world for AI.

**(Scene 4 — The Uber-Rivian Robotaxi Invasion)**
Narrator: Uber is cutting out the middleman, dropping over a billion dollars into Rivian to deploy fifty-thousand robotaxis. The gig-worker age is hitting a wall as the world’s largest rideshare platform swaps human drivers for an autonomous fleet.

**(Scene 5 — Outro)**
Narrator: Keep your eye on the boring stuff.

*   **Physics Check:** Simple vertical reveals and steady pans ensure stable, high-quality motion.
*   **Motion Plan:** Vertical tilts and deep-focus pushes capitalize on the 9:16 vertical frame.

**Guidelines:** All scenes utilize a "Miniature Model / Diorama" aesthetic with visible textures, handcrafted props, and soft tabletop lighting. No on-screen text.

**Scene 2 — The Qatar LNG Inferno**

- **Motion Prompt:** A slow camera push-in toward the neck of the massive hourglass, focusing on the slow, single drops of blue liquid as the refinery blurs into a soft glow in the background.

- **Motion Prompt:** A slow camera pan from the smooth, windowless robotaxi to the lone man holding the useless steering wheel.
